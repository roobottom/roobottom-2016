---
title: Bricks
---
A `bricks` grid, for laying out multiples of 10 objects with a "brick wall" effect.

## Example

```
pattern.grid(
  ['<div class="eg">brick</div>',
  '<div class="eg">brick</div>',
  '<div class="eg">brick</div>',
  '<div class="eg">brick</div>',
  '<div class="eg">brick</div>',
  '<div class="eg">brick</div>',
  '<div class="eg">brick</div>',
  '<div class="eg">brick</div>',
  '<div class="eg">brick</div>',
  '<div class="eg">brick</div>'],
  modifier='bricks'
)
```
